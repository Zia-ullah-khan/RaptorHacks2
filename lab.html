MC Hackathon 2026

RaptorHacks is Montgomery College's premier hackathon, hosted by the AI Club and Raptor Who Code. Open exclusively to current MC students, teams of 2–4 will build and present innovative projects across 7 tracks. The in-person Demo Day takes place April 11, 2026 at the Germantown campus. Each team can submit their MVP under up to 3 tracks.

With over $4K in prizes, free lunch, swag bags from igniteHub, and judging by industry professionals, this is your chance to hack, compete, and showcase your skills. The GenAI track features both a virtual submission (MVP + 5-min demo video by March 22) and an in-person component on Demo Day. All teams must be present to be judged, except the AI virtual track.
